Empire Assault

Empire Assault is a tower defence game with strategic resource gathering elements to protect your empire against evil armies.

Develop your defences
into a formidable power
to defeat the evil wizard

Gathering Resources

Resource tiles are harvested giving you resources and land to build your new defences.

Constructing Defences

On empty land tiles build exciting defences to defeat the approaching army.

Defeating Evil

The evil wizard has created a magical path to your castle and only you can defeat his army with an even stronger one!
